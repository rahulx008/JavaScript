DataTypes in JS
- Number: Used for both integers and floating-point numbers.
- BigInt: Represents very large integers beyond the safe limit of Number.
- String: A sequence of characters used for text.
- Boolean: Represents logical values: either true or false.
- undefined: A variable that has been declared but not assigned a value.
- null: Represents an intentional absence of any object value.
- Symbol: A unique and immutable identifier, often used as object keys.
